Description

VISH is a Brazilian company based in Bahia, founded in 2022. The studio develops games for Steam and has recently expanded into the mobile market. Its focus is on creating fun, engaging games with distinctive designs. The company collaborates with a publisher to distribute its games in Asia while self-publishing in other regions.

History

VISH Game Studio was born from a group of friends who met during their computer science studies. After spending six years participating in game jams together, they took their first major step into the gaming industry by launching a project on Steam in 2021. This milestone marked the beginning of their journey as a professional game studio.

With a strong focus on character design, engaging mechanics, and delivering fun, high-quality experiences, VISH has since grown into a studio dedicated to crafting unique and exciting games for players worldwide.

Notably, VISH was one of the ten Latin American studios selected for the Google Indie Fund 2024, an investment program by Google aimed at supporting growing game studios.
