Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome! This page outlines how we at VISH GAME STUDIO ("we" or "us") handle your personal data when you ("you") play Just King (the "Game").

Last Updated: November 7, 2024

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be communicated to you directly within the Game.

Data Collection and Usage

To provide the Game experience, we need to collect and process certain information about you. Below, we explain what data we collect and how we use it.

User-Provided Data

The following information is provided by you and stored on our servers:

UserID (All Platforms): Necessary for sign-up.

User-Generated Data

As you play the Game, additional data is generated, including information on matches you've played, party compositions, items owned, and similar game activities.

Some of this data, such as leaderboard rankings and party compositions, may be visible to other players.

We store and process this information to enhance your gaming experience and to allow features like leaderboards and multiplayer interactions.

Legitimate interest

We collect and analyze user-generated data to:

Inform future game updates and balance patches

Assess overall game performance

For data solely used in analytics, we retain it for a maximum of one month. (Data solely used in analytics is anonymized before being stored.)

Third-Party Data Privacy

The Game uses certain third-party services that may collect information to identify you:

Unity Services (All Platforms): The Game is built using Unity, which collects data for crash reports and bug tracking. Information collected includes a unique installation-specific ID, device name, operating system version, Game configuration at the time of an error, and usage statistics.

Please review Unity’s Privacy Policy for more details.

Data Security

We prioritize the protection of your data. All data transmissions are encrypted and stored in secure locations with strict access controls. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection of your information.

External Links

The Game may contain links to external websites not operated by us. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.
